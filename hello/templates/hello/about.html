Background

I also had an interest in science from a very young age. I dreamed about going to space, I watched Star Wars all the time, I even wanted to be an astronaut. Deep down I always knew I wanted to be an engineer too however. I am not entirely sure how a 6 year old tells their mother they want to be an engineer but I did. By the time I was in high school I decided I pursue a degree in computer science/ software engineering.


Today, I am studying Software Rngineering at Kennesaw State University here in Georgia, very close to home. My interest and love for computing has only grown since coming to college in the fall of 2020. Thank you for being apart of my journey. It really does mean a lot to me! Stay tuned to see what the future holds for me!
